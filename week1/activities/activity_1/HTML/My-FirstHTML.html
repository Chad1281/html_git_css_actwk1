How to make a sandwich

  1. Place 2 slices of bread on a plate.
  2. Spread mayonnaise on 1 slice of the bread.
  3. Place 3 slices of turkey on the other slice of bread.
  4. Place a slice of provolone cheese on the slices of turkey.
  5. Place a leaf of lettuce on top of the provolone.
  6. Place a tomato slice on top of the lettuce.
  7. Place the first slice of bread mayonnaise side down on top of the tomato.
